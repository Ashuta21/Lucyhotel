About Us

Lucy Hotel is One of the The Best Hotels in Amhara Region, Dessie City Ethiopia. Conveniently located in the center of The City at Menafesha road, only 23 kms away from Kombolcha Airport. Lucy Hotel has 64 spacious and elegantly furnished guest rooms, beautiful and comfy bar, With restaurant, Gymnasium and convenient Parking Our Hotel is Private owned Hotel dedicated to providing excellent accommodations that allow Customers to relax in the stylish comfort and feel of their own home while on a long or short stay in Dessie City.
